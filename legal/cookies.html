Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. This policy provides detailed information about how we use cookies and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file saved on your device when you visit a site. It allows the site to remember your actions and preferences over time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them.

Detailed Cookie Usage

The table below lists the primary cookies we use. Note that third-party services like Google Analytics may set their own cookies, which are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Cookie Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Stores your consent choices. (Necessary) 1 Year
_ga, _gid Distinguishes users for analytics. (Performance) 2 Years
user_prefs Remembers choices like language. (Functionality) 6 Months

How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish using the settings on this page or in your browser. For more details, visit aboutcookies.org. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.
