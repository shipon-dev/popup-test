1. Introduction

"Welcome to Ground State Coffee. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us."

This privacy notice describes how we might use your information if you visit our website, engage with us in other related ways, including any sales, marketing, or events. In this privacy notice, if we refer to "we," "us," or "our," we are referring to Ground State Coffee.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, as well as information that is automatically collected when you use our services. Here’s a breakdown of what we gather.

Information You Provide

  • Contact: Name, email, address, phone.
  • Account: Username and password.
  • Payment: Securely processed card info.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device: IP address, browser, OS.
  • Usage: Pages visited, time on site.
  • Location: Country derived from IP.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for legitimate business purposes to provide and improve our services. Key uses include:

Account Management

To create and manage your account.

Order Fulfillment

To process payments and deliver orders.

Communications

To send marketing and service updates.

Service Improvement

To analyze usage and improve our offerings.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with trusted third parties only in the following limited circumstances to provide and improve our services.

Service Providers

With partners who perform services for us (e.g., payment processing, shipping).

Legal Compliance

If required by law or to protect rights and prevent fraud.

5. Data Security

Our Commitment to Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information. While we strive for 100% security, no system is impenetrable. We continuously work to strengthen our safeguards.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have rights regarding your personal information. Depending on your location, these may include the following:

  • The Right to Access – You can request copies of your personal data.
  • The Right to Rectification – You can request that we correct any inaccurate information.
  • The Right to Erasure – You can request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

7. Changes to This Policy

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent version. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
