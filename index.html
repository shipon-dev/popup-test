OUR JOURNEY

From a Hypothesis to Your Cup

Ground State Coffee began as a simple hypothesis: what if we treated coffee not just as a drink, but as an experience in precision and balance? Founded by two friends with a shared passion for science and caffeine, our journey started in a small garage, experimenting with roasting profiles and brewing methods.

Today, our "lab" is a welcoming space for everyone. We're dedicated to crafting the perfect cup and building a community where ideas can be shared, and focus can be found.
